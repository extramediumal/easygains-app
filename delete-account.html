Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 6, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your EasyGains account and all associated data using either method below:

Option 1: In the App

  1. Open EasyGains
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm when prompted

Option 2: By Email

If you no longer have access to the app, send a deletion request to easygainsapp@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Profile data (goals, targets, preferences)
  • All meal and food logs
  • All workout logs
  • Macro and calorie history
  • Coach AL reactions and notification history

What Happens to Your Subscription

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. If you have an active EasyGains Pro subscription, cancel it first through your device's app store (App Store or Google Play) to avoid future charges.

Timeline

Account deletion is processed immediately when done in-app. Email requests are processed within 30 days.
